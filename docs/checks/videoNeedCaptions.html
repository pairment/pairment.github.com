--- layout: docs title: Videos need closed captions ---

All videos need captions for users who are deaf or hard of hearing. Captions should align with the audio track of the video.

Information privacy:

This check sends information back to our servers, but we do not retain that information.

When a video is found on the page, we send just the video URL to our testing service. Once the video is checked, we do not retain the data.

Guideline information:

This check applies to WCAG 2.0 Sections:
  • 1.2.2
  • 1.2.4
and WCAG techniques:
  • G87
