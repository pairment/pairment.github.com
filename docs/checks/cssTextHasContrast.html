--- layout: docs title: Text should have appropriate contrast ---
Text should have high enough contrast to be readable. If your text were a light grey on a white background, it would be difficult for most people to read. Users with low vision or color blindness need high contrast to read text.

Guideline information:

This check applies to WCAG 2.0 Sections:
  • 1.3.1
and WCAG techniques:
  • G18
