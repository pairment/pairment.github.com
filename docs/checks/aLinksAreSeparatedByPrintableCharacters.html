--- layout: docs title: Lists of links should be seperated by printable characters ---
Lists of links should be structured into lists or other equivalent elements. Links separated by non-readable characters (i.e. "|") can lead to confusing about the relationship of the links to one another. For example:   
<p><a href="/map">Map of our office</a> | <a href="/contact">Contact us</a></p>
 

Guideline information:

None identified
