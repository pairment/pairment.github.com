--- layout: docs title: Image alternative text is not too short ---

Image alternative text should be descriptive. While this is difficult to measure, in general we recommend an alternative text of no less than 10 characters. In pairment, you can change that minimum character count in the check settings to suit your needs.

Guideline information:

This check applies to WCAG 2.0 Sections:
  • 1.1.1
and WCAG techniques:
  • H37
