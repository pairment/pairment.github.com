--- layout: docs title: Image alternative text should be descriptive ---
All images with alternative text should describe the image. Avoid placeholders such as "image" or "picture." We consider the following words to be placeholders rather than real descriptive text:
  • image
  • title
  • untitled
  • untitled document
  • the title
  • content
  • [empty space]
  • new page
  • new
  • nbsp
  • spacer
  • img
  • photo
  • frame
  • frame title
  • iframe
  • iframe title
  • legend

Guideline information:

This check applies to WCAG 2.0 Sections:
  • 1.1.1
  • 1.2.1
and WCAG techniques:
  • F30
  • F39
