--- layout: docs title: Image alternative text is not too long ---

Image alternative text should be clear and concise. While this is difficult to measure, in general we recommend an alternative text of no more than 100 characters. In pairment, you can change that maximum character count in the check settings to suit your needs.

Guideline information:

This check applies to WCAG 2.0 Sections:
  • 1.1.1
and WCAG techniques:
  • H37
