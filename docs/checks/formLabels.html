--- layout: docs title: Form elements need labels ---
Users with a screen reader cannot tell what form elements (like text input or checkboxes) mean unless they are explicitly labeled. Use either a label tag, aria-label, or aria-labeledby instead.

Guideline information:

This check applies to WCAG 2.0 Sections:
  • 1.1.1
  • 1.3.1
  • 2.1.1
  • 2.1.3
  • 3.3.2
  • 4.1.2
and WCAG techniques:
  • H44
  • F68
  • H91
