Privacy policy

Pairment on your site

When you have Pairment installed on your site (by including a javascript tag on your pages), Pairment runs entirely on your user's browser. With the exception outlined below, no information, content, metadata, or any other data is sent back to Pairment.

Certain checks cannot be run in a browser. For example, checking for the presence of captions in a YouTube video. You will be warned when adding these checks to your site that information is shared with Pairment, and whether that information is retained. In all cases, we strive to only send the minimum amount of information needed back to our servers in order to run the check successfully

When you log in

When you log into the Pairment service, the information you provide (your username, email, billing information, site information, and check preferences) are stored and retained by Pairment for the use of running the service. This information is not, nor will it ever be, shared, sold, or given to any third party without your consent.
